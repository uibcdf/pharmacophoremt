# Modeling Strategies

Fundamental approaches for pharmacophore generation.

## 1. Ligand-Based Modeling
- Generation from molecular sets, alignments, and common feature extraction.

## 2. Structure-Based (Receptor) Modeling
- Generation from receptor pockets (via `topomt`), water networks, and interaction fields.

## 3. Complex-Based Modeling
- Generation from protein-ligand interactions, including static crystal structures and MD trajectories.

## 4. Hybrid Approaches
- Merging information from multiple sources (e.g., enriching a ligand-based model with receptor pocket constraints).
